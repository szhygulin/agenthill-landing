Moat

Hub economics that compound

Channel networks have one structural moat: liquidity network effects. They also have one structural problem: bootstrapping hubs when capital is locked and unproductive. AgentHill solves the second to unlock the first.

LIGHTNING (BTC)
Routing fees only
BTC has no native yield. Lightning hubs earn fractions of a basis point on locked capital. Network has stayed at ~5,000 BTC capacity for years.
Result: retail UX forced custodian collapse (WoS, Strike). No middle tier of hubs.
AGENTHILL (STABLECOINS)
Routing fees + DeFi yield
Stablecoin collateral earns 3–5% APY in Aave/Compound via share-based proxy contracts. Hubs viable at far lower volume.
Result: many medium hubs, real routing topology, agents (not humans) as the endpoint.
